Transforming Trauma into Thriving

For years, I have had the privilege of serving powerful men and women who have survived childhood abuse, sexual assault, and domestic violence. Despite their vast differences in backgrounds all of the people I serve have been determined to see what their lives would look like if they lived from a place of intention, power, and authenticity. Together, we work together to identify and revise the limitingbeliefs that you were forced to align with in order to survive trauma. My social justice oriented counseling will assist you in healing from trauma while also decreasing the likelihood of re-traumatization that is often a reality for manysurvivors.
